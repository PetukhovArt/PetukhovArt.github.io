Desert Eagle

Description

Magnum Research Desert Eagle Mark XIX Pistol .44 Magnum 6" Barrel SS, Muzzle Brake 8rd

Performance characteristics

Gas-operated, rotating bolt, carbon steel construction. Single action trigger with approximately four pound pull. Full picatinny rail on the barrel from end of chamber to right behind the front sight. Combat type, fixed sights. Polygonal rifling with right hand twist, 6 lands and grooves. Standard ambidextrous safeties. Includes muzzle brake.
